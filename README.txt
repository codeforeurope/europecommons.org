About europecommons.org
-----------------------

europecommons.org is a website that provides a catalogue of civic apps and
services for better governments, with a focus on open innovation. It is
built on OpenCivic.


ABOUT OPENCIVIC
---------------

OpenCivic (http://getopencivic.com/) is a Drupal distribution tailored
for creating app catalogues and hackathon websites.


ABOUT DRUPAL
------------

Drupal is an open source content management platform supporting a variety of
websites ranging from personal weblogs to large community-driven websites. For
more information, see the Drupal website at http://drupal.org/, and join the
Drupal community at http://drupal.org/community.
